  ABOUT

The Institute

The Lalit Narayan Mithila University is a public university in India ,established in 1972. The university initially functioned are from the Mohanpur House at Sara Mohanpur village of Darbhanga-Sakriroute. In 1975, it was shifted to the campus belonging to Raj Darbhanga.

The university is in Darbhanga town, Mithila, an ancient cultural region of North the India lies between the lower range of the Himalayas and the Ganges river. The university to imparts education in fields such as humanities, social science, life science, commerce,medicine,with both undergraduate and postgraduate courses. More than 400 faculties are engaged in teaching and research.
