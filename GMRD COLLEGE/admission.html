Admission

G.M.R.D College offers education from the Intermediate level to the under-Graduate level for boys & girls students in both Arts & Science Stream. All applicants seeking admission to the College should read the Bulletin of Information/Prospectus issued by the College. The minimum eligibility requirements for admission to various courses shall be specified in that bulletin.


Allocated seats shall be filled in the Colleges for admission purely on the basis of marks/counselling as per the University/State Government rules.Admission to a courses will be closed once the seats are filled.

Admission Procedure

Students passing out senior secondary examination of Bihar School Examination Board, Patna or its equivalent exam in the current or earlier session may
  apply for admission.

Admission to various Degree courses is strictly on the merit of the Applicant in the qualifying exam. (+2 or as the case may be) following reservation
  policy/rules of the Government of Bihar.

A selected candidate at the time of admission is required to submit /produce original mark sheet., S.L.C/C.L.C, Character Certificate, Migration
   Certificate (for the student who passing +2 level exam from a Board other than Bihar Examination Board, Patna), Cast and Income Certificate(wherever
   applicable) and   a selfaddressed duly stamped envelope. He/ She must pay his/her admission and other requisite fee as specified.
